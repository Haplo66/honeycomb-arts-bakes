# RIPPLE Bakes & Makes — Brand Identity

## Official Brand Name

RIPPLE Bakes & Makes


## Short Brand Name

RIPPLE


## Brand Descriptor

Bakes & Makes


## Brand Naming Rules

Use:

RIPPLE Bakes & Makes

Do not use legacy:

- Honeycomb Arts & Bakes
- Honeycomb
- Honeycomb Arts
- Honeycomb Bakery


## Brand Concept

RIPPLE Bakes & Makes represents handmade creativity through two complementary areas:

### Bakes

Handcrafted bakery creations including:

- Cookies
- Breads
- Filled pockets
- Custom baked goods


### Makes

Handmade sewn and crafted products including:

- Custom shirts
- Hats
- Baby products
- Rice packs
- Creative handmade items


## Brand Personality

RIPPLE should feel:

- Warm
- Handmade
- Creative
- Friendly
- Personal
- Welcoming


Avoid:

- Corporate styling
- Industrial feeling
- Generic marketplace appearance
